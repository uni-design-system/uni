## Overview

Navigation drawer with two modes sharing one content slot:

- **`side`** renders an in-flow `<aside>` that pushes page content — the classic
  dashboard sidenav. Opening and closing animates its width; a divider border primitive
  separates it from the content, and it is hidden from assistive tech while closed.
- **`over`** renders a native `<dialog>` in the top layer: focus trapping, Escape, and
  the scrim backdrop come from the platform, and the panel slides in from its edge
  (`position`: `start` or `end`).

`[(open)]` is two-way; Escape and backdrop clicks keep it in sync. Surface, width,
divider, elevation, padding and backdrop all resolve from the theme's `drawer` options.
