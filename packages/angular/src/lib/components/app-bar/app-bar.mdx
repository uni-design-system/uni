## Overview

Horizontal application bar for the top of a page or section. Project controls with the
`leading` attribute (menu button, logo) and actions with `trailing` (pushed to the far
edge); pass `title` or project your own center content. Surface color, height, the
divider rule, title typography and spacing all resolve from the theme's `appBar`
options, and `sticky` pins the bar to the top of its scroll container.

Place it inside your page's `<header>` landmark. It pairs with the Drawer — see the
Drawer docs for the full dashboard-shell recipe.
