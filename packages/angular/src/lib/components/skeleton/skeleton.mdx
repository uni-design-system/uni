## Overview

Loading placeholder that sketches the shape of incoming content: `text` renders one or
more lines (ending on a short line, as real copy would), `rect` stands in for media and
charts, `circle` for avatars. Skeletons paint with the theme's surface tokens so they
sit naturally on any brand in both modes, and the shimmer sweep only animates for users
who allow motion — under `prefers-reduced-motion` it degrades to calm static blocks.

Skeletons are `aria-hidden`; announce loading on the container (e.g. `aria-busy`)
where your app knows what is actually loading.
